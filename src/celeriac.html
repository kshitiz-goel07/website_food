Tips for cooking with celeriac



1. Know What to Look for in Celeriac

Choose small, firm celeriac that is heavy for its size. Avoid any celery root with soft spots and a lot of rootlets.

2. Keep It Cool

Refrigerate whole celeriac in a plastic bag for up to a week.

3.prevent Discoloration

Before using celery root, peel and soak it briefly in water with a little vinegar or lemon juice to prevent cut surfaces from darkening.
